Current position¬∂

Last edited, June 14th, 2022

I am a post-doctoral researcher at the Department of Geosciences of the University of Padova and affiliated to Earth & Environmental Sciences Area at Lawrence Berkeley National Laboratory (LBL), where my research focuses on the application of geophysical methods to energy and environment related topics, including:

  • hydro-and bio-geophysical modelling of subsurface hydrological processes;

  • the development of novel imaging approaches for plant root and root-soil interactions.

During my stay at LBL in the I have been working on Data Assimilation and processing of long term soil plant interaction monitoring.
